ABOUT THE PRACTICE

Example Dental Clinic provides comprehensive, personalized dental care to patients of all ages in St. John's and the surrounding communities. Our team combines clinical excellence with a genuinely warm patient experience — from the first phone call to every follow-up visit.

We believe modern dentistry should be approachable, transparent, and convenient. That means clear communication about treatment options, honest conversations about cost, and access to care when you need it — including same-day appointments for urgent concerns.

Whether you're due for a cleaning, considering cosmetic work, or dealing with a dental emergency, our goal is the same: helping you maintain a healthy, confident smile for life.

Our front desk is supported by an AI assistant that answers patient questions 24/7 — so when you need hours, insurance details, or emergency guidance outside business hours, help is just a message away.
